About me:-

My name is kamlesh Bisht, I am from Nainital, India from the Himalayan region, though I completed MBM (Marketing Business Management); I am currently working with barefoot college,Tilonia from last 4 years in SOLAR R&D (Research and Development department). I enjoy music,traveling, dancing, building ideas, meeting new people. I also like to ensure that what ever I do will at least benefit someone.

To share a bit about Barefoot College Barefoot college, like all groundbreaking, influential places, this special place started out as a simple idea. Acting upon that small idea got Barefoot College going into the world to solve various problems. Being at the R&D department in Barefoot College I have found the need for powerful tools to execute the ideas in physical working technology, that is why college decided to set up a Fab Lab. I was delighted to hear the news about fab lab in my own place and there was also an announcement that someone will be selected to do the fab academy to help set up the lab in college.

Though I was not from an engineering background, I really have the interest to study about fab lab and its network.After completing the course, I will help to set up a Fab Lab in Barefoot College , which I believe will also help lots of other people to learn about the beauty of this fab lab network. I would also like to take the role of instructor in Barefoot college. One of the main idea of fab lab that I really liked is the idea of sharing things with the world and I would like to be as much of a help to this network.
